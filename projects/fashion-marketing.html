Power BI

Marketing Spend & Sales Performance — Fashion Retail

Power BI · Star Schema · WoW ROAS Analysis

A Star Schema model integrating 10,500+ records across 4 tables with dynamic DAX measures for Week-over-Week ROAS, Budget, and Revenue Growth. The dashboard diagnosed underfunded hero SKUs and low-ROAS campaigns — enabling data-driven marketing budget reallocation.

📅 2024 🛠️ Power BI, DAX 📊 10,500+ Records · 4 Tables

01. Business Challenges

A fashion retailer was spending aggressively on marketing across multiple channels — but had no way to measure which campaigns and product categories were actually delivering a positive Return on Ad Spend (ROAS).

Marketing budgets were allocated based on historical patterns rather than performance data, resulting in underfunded hero SKUs and overspending on low-performing campaigns.

💸 Budget Misallocation

Marketing spend was distributed evenly across campaigns — with no performance-based reallocation to top-performing channels or products.

📉 No ROAS Tracking

The team had no unified view of Return on Ad Spend at the campaign, channel, or SKU level — making optimization impossible.

🛍️ Underfunded Heroes

Best-selling SKUs with strong organic demand were receiving the same marketing investment as underperformers — a missed growth opportunity.

02. My Approach

I built a comprehensive marketing performance dashboard that connects spend data to revenue outcomes — enabling the team to see exactly which investments are paying off and which are burning budget.

1

Star Schema Design

Integrated 10,500+ records from 4 source tables into a clean star schema — fact tables for transactions and marketing spend, dimension tables for products and campaigns.

2

Dynamic DAX Measures

Created Week-over-Week (WoW) comparison measures for ROAS, Budget Efficiency, Revenue Growth, and Conversion Rate — with time-intelligence functions for trending analysis.

3

Actionable Dashboards

Designed interactive pages covering Marketing Overview, Campaign Performance, Product-Level ROAS, and Budget Allocation recommendations.

04. Key Insights

The analysis uncovered clear opportunities for budget reallocation and campaign optimization:

🏆 Hero SKU Opportunity

Top-selling SKUs with strong organic demand were significantly underfunded — increasing their marketing spend could unlock outsized revenue growth.

📉 Low-ROAS Campaigns

Several campaigns were delivering ROAS below 1.0× — meaning the company was losing money on every dollar spent in these channels.

📈 WoW Revenue Growth

Week-over-Week analysis revealed seasonal patterns in campaign effectiveness — suggesting optimal timing windows for budget bursts.

💡 Budget Efficiency

Reallocating just 20% of budget from low-ROAS to high-ROAS campaigns could significantly improve overall marketing ROI.

05. Business Impact

The dashboard gave the marketing team a data-driven framework for making budget decisions — replacing guesswork with measurable outcomes.

🎯 Performance-Based Budgets

Shift from historical allocation to ROAS-based budgeting — investing more where returns are proven.

🛍️ Fund the Winners

Increase marketing spend on hero SKUs that show strong organic demand and high conversion rates.

🔄 Weekly Optimization

Use WoW tracking to make weekly budget adjustments — catching declining campaigns early and scaling winners fast.
